Vanilla cake batter

Description

Plush, moist, fluffy crumb without being freakishly unnaturally so (as some store bought can be). No more dense cakes!

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • milk
  • sugar
  • butter
  • vanilla
  • oil
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs with sugar for a whole 7 minutes until thick and glossy, it changes from yellow to white, and tripled in volume
  2. Whisk together the flour, baking powder and salt. Then add into egg mixture in 3 lots, mixing for just 5 seconds on Speed 1 in between. This should make the flour just incorporated
  3. Add hot milk and melted butter into this.
  4. Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs. And batter is done.
