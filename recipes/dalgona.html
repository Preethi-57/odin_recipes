Dalgona

Description

Dalgona is a South korean street candy which has gained great popularity recently due to the netfilx series "Squid Games". It is usually prepared by old street vendors right in front of the customer.
Dalgon usually has a shape on it, which if you are successful to get the right shapw without breaking, the vendor will give you another Dalgona for free.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Baking soda

Steps

  1. Take a bowl, add 3 tablespoons of sugar to it and put it on low flame.
  2. After the Sugar melts, add a pinch of baking soda and stir vigorously.
  3. After it puffs up a bit, pour the dalgona onto a parchment paper.
  4. Press it softly with a flat object and punch any shape you want.
    5. Let it cool and the Dalgona is your's to devour.
